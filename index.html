Booking Follow-Up Rules
Auto-generate To-Do tasks when booking form answers match your conditions
⚡ How Booking Rules Work
Your bookings script returns all customer form answers (e.g. Dash Cam Brand, Parking Notes, Car Condition, etc.) as columns in your sheet. Rules scan every new booking and — if the answer matches your condition — automatically add a task to the Team To-Do list, assigned to whoever you specify.

Example: Field dash_cam_brand → Condition equals "Other" → Task: "Follow up: confirm customer's dash cam model before booking" → Assign to Sarah

Rules run automatically whenever bookings refresh. Click Run Rules Now to force a check.
